Location

Šimuni

Our house is situated in Šimuni and it's the place where you will be staying! Šimuni is a quiet and peaceful fishing village located on the beautifully unique island of Pag in the Adriatic sea. It's an ideal place if you want to spend your holidays in a calm, beautiful area, surrounded by the sea. Šimuni is in a remote location but it's still in close proximity to towns Novalja and Pag. There are many places that you can visit on the island. Have your holiday in Šimuni and experience wonderful beaches, crystal clear sea, a marina, extraordinary restaurants, beautiful diving and hiking locations, incredible views and much, much more.

Island of Pag

Island of Pag belongs to the north Dalmatian archipelago and it extends northwest-southeast along the coast, forming the Velebit channel. The two biggest cities on the island are Pag and Novalja which are in close proximity to Šimuni and the apartments. Pag is an island full of history. The Romans made their presence felt in the 1st century BC and some of the architecture can still be seen today. Pag also hosts the first Croatian wind farm. Besides that Pag was connected throughout history to salt production and cattle breeding, mainly sheep, and the tradition never stopped. The result is the fenomenal Pag cheese which you can taste and buy on the island. For more information on what to do on the island check out the official website: Visit Pag.

Novalja & Zrće beach

Novalja and Pag are the two biggest cities on the island. Novalja is a tourist destination best known for its crazy parties and clubbing scene because of the famous Zrće beach which is located near it. If you want to have a crazy night out one day while you're staying here, feel free to check out some of the best music festivals and an amazing atmosphere there. For more information on parties and festivals visit Zrće beach.

Town of Pag

The town of Pag is the biggest town on the island and it has lots of tourist attractions which makes it a great destination to visit during the summer. You will find a rich cultural and gastronomical heritage there. It is surrounded by beautiful beaches and an interesting rocky landscape. You can learn more about Pag here: Town of Pag.

Activities

Sail with us on our boat!

Šimuni has a lot of beaches and bays around it and while most of them are accessible by car or by foot there are some hidden gems that you can't visit without a boat. Take a journey with us and sail on our boat and experience all the beauty of Pag archipelago. We'll take you to the uninhabited island of Maun where you'll get to swim in one of the most beautiful secluded bays in the Adriatic sea. If we're lucky we might even catch some fish that we can later prepare for you on our grill.

Explore beautiful beaches

The island of Pag is famous for its beautiful landscape, especially its beaches. One of the most amazing ones is located in Šimuni, just 500 meters away from the apartments. There are countless other beaches im Šimuni but they are more secluded and private. We can tell you how to access them when you come here on your holiday! Besides the beaches in Šimuni, there are other extraordinary beaches on the island. For example Bošana, Čista and Ručica to name a few. Check out this list of popular beaches on the island: Pag beaches.

Old olive trees in Lun

Lun is the northernmost town on the island. It's a small, interesting town settled in a small bay. The most intersting thing about the town of Lun are its magnificent olive trees which are considered to be the oldest olive trees in the world.You can check out more interesting things about Lun and its famous olive trees on their website.

Scuba diving in Šimuni

Šimuni has a diving center which is very close to our house in Camp Šimuni. If you are interested in adventure or you just want to explore the depths around the island, feel free to check it out. Visit their offical website here.

Konoba Didova Kuća

Konoba Didova Kuća is our restaurant which is located in the first house ever built in the village just below the apartments where you will be staying. With its colorful design, and traditional dishes made with fresh and local ingredients it offers a very special dining experience.

Pag cheese

We have a small flock of sheep and we make our own Pag cheese which you can try. If you are interested in the tradition behind Pag cheese you can try all sorts of Pag cheese around the island. One of the more famous tours where you can see how the cheese is made and taste some of it is held in the Gligora cheese factory.
